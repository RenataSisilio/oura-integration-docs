Privacy Policy - Arena Holdings Oura Integration

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Arena Holdings ("we", "us", or "Arena") handles data in connection with our internal Oura Ring integration. This integration is developed exclusively for internal use by Arena Holdings employees and is not available to the general public.

Scope

Internal Use Only: This integration is for internal use only by employees of Arena Holdings. We are not a consumer-facing service and do not have external customers or users.

What Data We Collect

Through the Oura API integration, we access the following types of data from employees who authorize the connection:

  • Sleep Data: Sleep stages, duration, sleep quality scores
  • Activity Data: Daily steps, calories burned, activity metrics
  • Readiness Data: Readiness scores, heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Heart Rate Data: Heart rate measurements throughout the day

This data is only collected when an employee explicitly authorizes the Oura integration through OAuth 2.0 authentication.

How We Use Your Data

The data accessed through this integration is used solely to:

  • Enable employees to query their own health metrics through our internal AI assistant (Claude)
  • Provide personalized health insights and analysis at the employee's request
  • Support employee wellness and productivity optimization

We do not:

  • Share health data between employees without explicit consent
  • Use health data for employment decisions or performance reviews
  • Sell or share health data with third parties
  • Use health data for advertising or marketing purposes

Data Storage and Security

  • Token Storage: OAuth access tokens are stored securely in our internal Notion database with encryption at rest
  • Data Retention: We store only the authentication tokens required for API access, not the health data itself
  • Data Minimization: Health data is retrieved from Oura's servers only when requested by the employee and is not permanently stored by us
  • Access Control: Only the employee who authorized the connection can access their own health data through the integration

Employee Rights

Employees have the right to:

  • Revoke Access: Disconnect the Oura integration at any time by revoking OAuth permissions in their Oura account settings
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of stored authentication tokens at any time
  • No Retaliation: Use or non-use of this integration has no impact on employment status or evaluation

Third-Party Services

This integration connects to:

  • Oura Ring API: Subject to Oura's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic's Claude: Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

Internal Use Statement

This integration is developed and maintained exclusively for internal use by Arena Holdings employees. We are a private investment firm and do not provide services to external customers or the general public. This integration is not commercially available and is not intended for distribution outside of Arena Holdings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Employees will be notified of significant changes through internal communication channels.
